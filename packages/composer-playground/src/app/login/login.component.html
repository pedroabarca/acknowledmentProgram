The Avantica's Blockchain

Avantica has a recognition system for the employees whose management is completely manual called the Costa Rica Recognition Program. This introduces the possibility of points accounting errors, or even the omission of acknowledgments by managers or the collaborators themselves.

On the other hand, there is no simple and self-service way for each employee to see their status (accumulated points) throughout the period. Additionally, there are no established controls for the inspection or assurance of recognition processes or points awarded to guarantee the reliability of the results in each period.

So the previous problem leads us to the need to develop an application based on Blockchain technology which aims to cover and solve the problem in an effective way, for this, through a Smart Contract that will seek to cover all regulations or rules that each of the 2 recognition categories possesses, all of these, are within the policies already defined by the Costa Rica Recognition Program developed by Avantica.

The projection of the tool is the development of a chain of blocks that allows a free, safe and honest transfer of acknowledgments (certificates to recognize the good work or attitude of a co-worker). The base of the network is blockchain, which gives us a degree of transfer and supervision that cannot be easily implemented in a centralized database.
